UNVEIL
Discipline over Motivation — a habit game that unveils a picture

Hang one picture you want to finish, then sign a contract: a duration and your daily pledges.
Each day you keep your word, a piece of the cover falls away.
Complete the journey, and the picture is yours.

Why

Motivation is fickle; discipline stays. Instead of cheers and badges, Unveil offers a contract with yourself. Today's pledges lock the moment you register them, and nothing changes them until the day ends. There is no punishment for a failed day — no pieces are revealed and your finish date slips by one, but the picture always waits for you.

How it works

1Hang a picture — your own photo, or a built-in artwork chosen by keywords only (a mystery until unveiled)
2Sign the contract — duration (7–365 days), daily pledges, reset hour, reveal mode
3Each morning — register and lock today's pledges (or let auto-renew do it for you)
4Through the day — check off pledges; the last check makes pieces cascade away
5Finish — a finale on the last day, a frame in your gallery, and a timelapse replay of the whole journey

In practice

A 100-day push-up challenge
Pledge “100 push-ups” and set auto-increase +1 — each completed day it grows to 101, 102… Reaching your target of 200 locks it in with a celebration. Failed days never raise the bar.
Studying for a vocabulary exam
With a “decide daily” goal, choose each day's word count to match your day, while every successful day unveils the picture dramatically by progress (cinematic mode).
Fixed routines — reading, workouts, meditation
Turn on auto-renew and your locked pledges become a standing contract, carried over daily even if you never open the app. Edits apply from tomorrow — today's lock never changes.

Principles

  • Honest verdicts — PERFECT / PARTIAL (exactly one short, never twice in a row) / FAILED / SKIPPED
  • Delay, not punishment — a failed day reveals nothing; the finish date slips, the picture is preserved
  • Local-first — no account, no server, no ads, no tracking. Not even the internet permission
  • Free analytics — golden hours, weekday patterns, per-pledge success, a shareable monthly report card — all computed on your device
  • Quiet notifications — they fire only when something is unfinished; a completed day stays silent
  • Activity calendar · daily logbook with your pledges and journal · gallery with timelapse replays
